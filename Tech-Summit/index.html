Code Of Conduct

1. Inclusivity and Respect
Treat all attendees, speakers, sponsors, and volunteers with kindness and respect.
Embrace diversity and inclusivity. Be open-minded and considerate of different backgrounds, perspectives, and experiences.
2. Harassment-Free Environment
Harassment and discrimination in any form will not be tolerated. This includes, but is not limited to, offensive comments, unwelcome advances, and inappropriate images or language.
If you experience or witness harassment, please report it immediately to event organizers or staff.
3. Collaborative and Supportive Atmosphere
Encourage collaboration and the sharing of ideas. Be supportive of fellow attendees' projects and initiatives.
Help create an environment where everyone feels comfortable asking questions and seeking assistance.
4. Responsible Technology Use
Use technology responsibly and ethically. Respect the privacy and security of others.
Avoid disruptive behaviour, such as excessive noise, during presentations and workshops.
5. Compliance with Local Laws and Policies
Comply with all applicable local laws and event policies.
Respect the venue and its rules and regulations.
6. Photography and Recording
Seek permission before taking photos or recording videos of individuals or their projects.
Respect the privacy of others who may not wish to be photographed or recorded.
7. Reporting Violations
If you witness a violation of this Code of Conduct, promptly report it to event organizers or staff.
Organizers will take appropriate action, which may include warning, expulsion from the event, or involvement of law enforcement if necessary.
8. Covid-19 Safety Guidelines
Follow any health and safety guidelines provided by event organizers, including those related to COVID-19.
Be mindful of social distancing and hygiene practices to ensure the well-being of all attendees.
9. Feedback and Improvement
Provide constructive feedback to help improve future events.
Encourage a culture of continuous improvement and learning.
